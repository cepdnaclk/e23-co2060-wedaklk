<!-- ABOUT THE PROJECT -->

About The Platform

Wedak.lk is a specialized peer-to-peer (P2P) service marketplace designed to modernize Sri Lanka's informal gig economy. We address the critical inefficiencies of finding reliable household service providers by connecting "Clients" (householders) directly with skilled "Service Providers" (freelancers/workers) in a secure digital environment.

Unlike traditional directories or classifieds, Wedak.lk operates on a competitive bidding model. This empowers users to negotiate fair market prices for tasks ranging from home repairs to daily chores, ensuring transparency and fairness for both parties.

<!-- BUSINESS MODEL -->

Business Model

Our revenue strategy is designed to lower the barrier to entry for users while ensuring platform sustainability:

Free Listings: Unlike traditional platforms, Clients can post service requirements completely free of charge.

Success-Based Commission: We charge a flat 5% commission on the final agreed bid amount. This fee is only applicable when a Client accepts a Service Provider's bid.

No Hidden Fees: We do not charge subscription fees or lead generation fees. We only earn when our users succeed.

<!-- KEY FEATURES -->

Key Features

🛡️ Mandatory KYC Compliance: We prioritize safety by requiring National Identity Card (NIC) verification for all Service Providers before they can bid.

🔨 Dynamic Task Posting: Clients post detailed job requirements with images, descriptions, and location data to attract accurate bids.

💰 Competitive Bidding Engine: Service Providers bid on tasks in real-time, allowing for dynamic price discovery based on job complexity.

📵 Anti-Circumvention Protocols: To protect platform integrity, contact details (Phone/WhatsApp) are strictly masked. They are only revealed to the counterparty after a bid is accepted and the service connection is finalized.

📍 Smart Location Matching: Integration with Google Maps API ensures Clients are matched with the nearest available providers to reduce travel costs.

🔒 Secure Transaction Handling: All platform fees are processed securely via the PayHere gateway.

<!-- TECHNOLOGY ARCHITECTURE -->

Technology Architecture

Wedak.lk is built on a modern, scalable MERN-based architecture to ensure high performance and reliability.

Frontend: Next.js 14 (React Framework) for Server-Side Rendering (SSR) and SEO.

Backend: Next.js API Routes / Node.js for serverless scalability.

Database: MongoDB Atlas for flexible data management of user profiles and bids.

Authentication: NextAuth.js for secure session management.

Integrations:

Payment: PayHere (Sri Lankan Payment Gateway)

SMS: Text.lk (Real-time notifications)

Maps: Google Maps API
